Modernisation in the age of AI

AI is transforming enterprise modernisation, making it faster, smarter, and more customer centric. In this engaging and thought-provoking webinar, experts from McKinsey & Company, Jason Forrest Agency, and Nearform share real-world perspectives on how AI and data visualisation are transforming the way organisations solve complex business problems. Explore emerging tools and strategies that put customer experience at the centre of transformation efforts—reducing the burden of replatforming and enabling smarter, more impactful tech decisions. Watch the recording to learn how leading teams are using AI to accelerate change and build for a post-legacy future. Date held: April 30, 2025

Our speakers

Srinivas Ramadath

McKinsey & Company Partner Srini is a Partner in McKinsey & Company with a long-standing career serving CEOs, business leaders and CIO’s to drive new avenues of growth enabled by digital and technology. Srini has a track record of incubating and driving end-to-end transformation programs by applying various problem solving techniques with a focus on areas like: Digital, Tech Strategy, AI and Gen AI, Platform Dev & modernization, API’s and Microservices, M&A in Tech and Change Management.

Tawanda Sibanda

McKinsey & Company Partner Tawanda Sibanda is a leader at Leap by McKinsey, helping major banks, insurers, and payment firms design, build, and scale new digital businesses. With 13+ years at McKinsey, he specializes in core technology modernization, digital platforms, and fintech innovation. Previously, he was a lead program manager at Microsoft. Tawanda holds S.B. and MEng degrees from MIT and an MBA (High Distinction) from Harvard Business School. His work includes leading platform modernization for top FinTechs, launching new digital businesses for banks, and driving financial inclusion through digital ecosystems.

Jason Forrest

Founder & McKinsey alum The Jason Forrest Agency Jason Forrest is a data visualization and design expert in NYC, working at the intersection of business, culture, and data. As the founder of the Jason Forrest Agency, he helps businesses solve complex problems through better data communication. Previously an Associate Partner at McKinsey, he led the Data Visualization Lab, launched the McKinsey Health Institute, and spearheaded the COVID Response Center. He is also the co-founder and editor-in-chief of Nightingale, the journal of the Data Visualization Society, and launched Nightingale Magazine, a global print publication. Jason is currently writing a book on communicating information in an uncertain world.

Helene Haughney

Nearform SVP for Delivery Transformation Hélène is SVP for Delivery Transformation at Nearform and GM for Global Accounts. With 30 years of experience in telecoms, IT, and high-tech industries, she specialises in driving complex product and service delivery at scale. A results-driven leader, she has a proven track record of exceeding targets, optimising service operations, and mentoring high-performing teams. Her expertise spans information development, strategic planning, and business growth through technology-driven transformation.

Kevin Devine

Nearform Design Director Kevin is a Design Director at Nearform, currently leading work in the public health sector. With over 19 years of experience, he specialises in discovery, design, and facilitation—shaping digital products from the ground up. He helps teams and stakeholders navigate complexity through structured discovery and collaborative design. His expertise in UX, product strategy, and user-centred design ensures practical, effective solutions that bridge business needs and user expectations.
