The power of open sourceThe power of open source_The power of open source_

Open source is part of how we work, not a marketing exercise. We contribute to raise engineering standards, encourage transparency, and deliver better outcomes for clients and communities.

Open source mindset

Contributions

Nearform actively encourages our team to get involved in open source in every meaningful way. From contributing to popular open source frameworks to our “sauce” hours programme that paves the way for our engineers to get involved in charity and community programmes, open source is at the core of what we do.

Global collaboration

Tapping into a pool of global talent means we learn from and with the world’s best developers. We take that knowledge and use it to build great digital solutions for ambitious enterprises.

Reuse, reduce

Mining open source lets us use components that have already been built, speeding up development and cutting down delivery time. It means we develop better software, faster.

Freedom to innovate

Working in an open environment gives us the freedom to innovate, getting rid of vendor lock-in, making software easy to integrate and giving clients more control over their ecosystem.

Future-proofing

Building with open source makes systems more transparent and secure, reducing bugs and vulnerabilities. So software lasts longer, costs less and performs better.

Open source projects

Node JS

Node.js is an Open Source, free, cross-platform, JavaScript runtime environment. Nearform has been instrumental to the development of Node.js since our foundation in 2011, and we’re proud to continue to be one of the most active global Node.js contributors today.

Learn more

Fastify

Fastify is a rapid, low-overhead web framework for Node.js that was created at Nearform. We continue to sponsor and drive its development and build its community — among our staff are Fastify project maintainers, contributors and even book authors.
