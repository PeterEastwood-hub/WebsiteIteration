Industry expertise. Enduring impact.Industry expertise. Enduring impact._Industry expertise. Enduring impact._

While Nearform’s best-in-class engineering can be deployed across a broad spectrum of enterprise challenges, over the past 15 years our experts have built unmatched domain expertise in a targeted set of industries. Pattern recognition is our strength, so we’re more focused on your AI maturity curve than our own learning curve. Nearform’s AI-native engineering approach is critical to client success, but it is not the only reason why the enterprises we partner with are also our biggest advocates. Our understanding of how to navigate within the complexity, and constraints, of each industry is built on years of real-world experience every member of the Nearform team brings on Day 1. We deliver across four industry areas: - Banking, financial services and insurance - Healthcare, life sciences, and public sector - Retail & e-commerce - Telecommunications & media

Banking, financial services & insurance

Our engagements with banking, financial services, and insurance companies leverage a robust knowledge base that includes regulatory requirements, experience in modernising complex legacy systems, managing data with high sensitivity, and designing user experiences that initiate and build customer trust.

Learn more

Healthcare, life sciences, & public sector

We serve clients in regulated industries like healthcare and life sciences, in both private and public sectors, whose missions involve delivering the best quality of care for healthy outcomes, meaning there is no room for error. Key to our speed to market is an understanding of regulatory requirements, complex clinical workflows, and an emphasis on data integrity.

Learn more

Retail & e-commerce

Depth in the retail and e-commerce industry means we take an omnichannel approach to design seamless customer experiences. We embrace data-driven and AI-enabled hyperpersonalisation for shoppers, while also making inventory and supply chain complexity easily and effectively manageable for employees.

Learn more

Telecommunications & media

Collaborating with network leaders in telecommunications and media requires experts who can keep pace with the rapid rate of technological innovation, ensure consistent delivery in a context of high customer expectations, manage regulatory oversight, and navigate complex multi-system environments.
