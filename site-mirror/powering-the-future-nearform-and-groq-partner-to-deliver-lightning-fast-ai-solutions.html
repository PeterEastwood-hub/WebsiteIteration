Powering the future: Nearform and Groq collaborate to deliver lightning-fast AI solutionsPowering the future: Nearform and Groq collaborate to deliver lightning-fast AI solutionsPowering the future: Nearform and Groq collaborate to deliver lightning-fast AI solutions

Discover how this collaboration gives enterprises AI applications that are reliable and rapid

Joe Szodfridt
8 Jul 2025
  • Share

Nearform is excited to announce a strategic collaboration with Groq that brings together the two companies' shared dedication to open source and pushing the boundaries of enterprise AI. Most technology partnerships end up being marketing exercises. This one isn’t.

Nearform’s partnership with Groq addresses a real problem: enterprise AI is often too slow for production use. We’ve all seen the demos where AI takes five to ten seconds to respond. That doesn’t work when you’re building applications that real people are using to get work done.

Groq has solved these problems. Its Language Processing Units (LPUs) deliver inference speeds that change what is possible — AI responses in milliseconds, not seconds. That is the difference between AI that disrupts workflows and AI that enhances them.

Here’s what the combination unlocks: Nearform can build enterprise applications where AI feels instantaneous. No more waiting, no more choosing between AI capability and user experience.

We’ve spent over a decade building resilient enterprise systems that can’t afford downtime. Groq’s infrastructure extends that same reliability to AI-powered applications. Its commitment to open-weight models aligns with Nearform’s open source principles — no vendor lock-in, no proprietary constraints.

This isn’t just about democratising AI for everyone. It’s about giving ambitious enterprises serious tools to build serious AI applications that actually improve how work gets done.

The technology finally matches the business need. That’s when real transformation happens.

The foundation: Groq’s revolutionary infrastructure

Most AI inference runs on GPUs that were designed for graphics, not language processing. It’s like using a race car to haul freight — it works, but it’s not optimal.

Groq built something different. Its Language Processing Units are purpose-built for AI inference from the ground up. The architecture colocates compute with memory on the same chip, eliminating the bottlenecks that plague GPU-based systems. The result is deterministic performance — when your request reaches the model, it executes predictably every time.

This matters more than just the benchmarks suggest. In enterprise environments, predictable performance means you can actually plan SLAs, set capacity and build reliable systems. When every instance runs with consistent timing, you can build applications that perform predictably under load.

The economics work too. Groq uses straightforward per-token pricing with no upfront costs. The API is OpenAI compatible, so switching takes three lines of code. No complex migration, no architectural rewrites.

GroqCloud’s flexibility fits how enterprises work. Its cloud platform gets you started fast and scales with your needs. For the cases where data sovereignty absolutely requires it, on-premises options exist. The cloud service is where you’ll see the fastest time-to-value and easiest, scalable deployments.

Partnership vision: Democratising enterprise AI

Many enterprise AI initiatives hit the same wall: the technology works in demos but struggles when deployed for actual work. The problem is often speed, but it’s also about predictable performance, cost control and deployment flexibility. When AI can’t deliver consistent results that fit within real enterprise constraints and operational requirements, adoption stalls.

Our partnership with Groq addresses that directly. Nearform is focused on building applications that solve real business problems reliably in production — AI that works when people need it to work.

The traditional trade-offs that force enterprises to compromise don’t apply here. You don’t have to choose between performance and cost when Groq’s architecture delivers both. You don’t have to choose between speed and capability when inference happens fast enough to support complex, multi-step reasoning. You don’t have to choose between innovation and control when open-weight models give you the flexibility to adapt without vendor restrictions.

Our shared principles are straightforward. Both companies believe open source leads to better outcomes. Both focus on making powerful technology accessible to organisations. Both prioritise building enduring solutions rather than chasing the latest trend.

This isn’t about replacing human expertise; it’s about giving that expertise tools to work at machine speed when the situation demands it.

Where our joint effort creates unique value: High-impact applications

The partnership delivers the greatest value where speed unlocks capabilities that weren’t practical before. These aren’t just faster versions of existing solutions; they are entirely new approaches that become viable when AI responds instantly:

Real-time decision support systems

This allows business teams to analyse complex data and get immediate insights during time-critical situations:

  • Contract negotiation teams can evaluate proposals from multiple vendors, company policies and other benchmarks during active discussions.
  • Retail recommendations can analyse customer behaviour, inventory levels and market trends to provide personalised product suggestions during active shopping sessions.
  • Language translations can instantly translate customer communications, support conversations and multilingual collaboration content during live interactions, ensuring seamless communications without conversational delays.

These solutions require immediate AI analysis and response during active business interactions. Groq’s speed ensures insights arrive within seconds, rather than creating awkward pauses that disrupt interactions.

Knowledge management systems

These let subject matter experts quickly capture, validate and distribute institutional knowledge across teams. AI helps synthesise expertise into actionable guidance without creating delays in critical business operations:

  • Industry insights and research platforms can analyse queries to provide enhanced search recommendations and related content during research sessions, helping users discover relevant content they might otherwise miss.
  • Knowledge base query and retrieval systems can search across documentation, policies and expertise repositories to provide contextual answers to support varied business processes.
  • Expert knowledge capture workflows can automatically convert meeting discussions, project debriefs and subject matter expert information into structured, searchable knowledge assets for future use.

Groq’s speed transforms knowledge management from a research bottleneck into real-time access, enabling teams to get answers fast and recommendations without pausing their work to wait for information retrieval and synthesis.

Business process automation with AI

This allows operations teams to build intelligent workflows that adapt and make decisions based on changing business conditions. These applications benefit from Groq’s speed to maintain workflow momentum and prevent bottlenecks:

  • Unstructured data processing workflows automatically extract, analyse and categorise information from documents, emails and text-based sources across business functions.
  • Content analysis and validation workflows evaluate submissions against complex business rules and contextual requirements, identifying patterns and exceptions that require attention.
  • Multi-system correlation workflows connect information across disparate systems to generate insights, identify relationships and ensure compliance with organisational policies.

These workflows leverage AI’s ability to understand context and meaning in textual data, enabling automation that goes beyond simple rule-based processing without waiting for lengthy analysis cycles.

Multi-step AI processes

These become practical when AI can complete complex reasoning chains quickly enough to fit within normal business timelines. These processes involve AI agents that can plan, execute multiple steps and adapt based on intermediate results:

  • Sequential analysis workflows where AI performs multiple rounds of analysis, validation and refinement before reaching conclusions.
  • Decision tree processing that branches and adapts based on intermediate results, requiring multiple AI calls to navigate complex business logic.
  • Multi-agent coordination workflows where different AI agents collaborate, each contributing specialised analysis that feeds into subsequent steps.

Groq’s speed advantage is transformative in these scenarios because latency compounds across each step in the chain. What is an acceptable two-second delay can become a 20-second wait when multiplied across a ten-step process. Groq's speed eliminates these compounding delays, making sophisticated agentic processes viable for real-world business applications.

Nearform and Groq: Building global enterprise AI leadership, together

Many AI partnerships are technology integrations. This one combines complementary business expertise to deliver enterprise AI implementations faster and more reliably.

Nearform brings over a decade of building enterprise systems that can’t fail. We understand the difference between a demo that works in controlled environments and a system that operates reliably under real business pressure. Our teams have built applications for organisations where downtime means loss of revenue, regulatory violations and/or operational disruption in mission-critical environments.

Groq provides the infrastructure foundation that makes enterprise AI applications practical. Its architecture delivers consistent performance characteristics that let us build applications with predictable behaviour. When we design systems for our clients, we can set realistic expectations and deliver on them.

The result is enterprise AI implementations that work reliably from day one, scale with business growth and integrate seamlessly with existing systems. We deliver solutions that bring measurable impact and meet enterprise requirements for security, compliance and operational excellence while taking advantage of breakthrough AI performance capabilities.

Conclusion: Enterprise AI that really works

The gap between AI demonstrations and production systems has kept many enterprises on the sidelines. The partnership between Nearform and Groq bridges the gap by combining breakthrough inference with proven enterprise integration expertise.

Enterprise AI succeeds when it falls naturally into existing business processes rather than requiring organisations to adapt to new technology constraints. The combination of Groq’s deterministic performance and Nearform’s multidimensional design approach creates AI that enhances productivity without disrupting established business flows.

Speed matters, but only when combined with reliability, security and seamless integration. The Nearform and Groq collaboration addresses all these challenges.

Our proven processes, built upon user-centered and domain-driven design foundations, address perspectives including user experience, technical performance, business integration, system modernisation and data architecture in parallel rather than in discrete silos. This multidimensional design approach enables rapid delivery of business value and systems that satisfy all perspectives without lengthy iteration cycles.

For organisations ready to move toward enterprise-wide AI deployment, this partnership offers a complete solution approach. We deliver implementations that scale with business growth, integrate with existing systems and maintain enterprise standards for security and compliance.

The future of enterprise AI belongs to practical applications that solve real business problems. This partnership is positioned to lead the transformation by building enterprise solutions that work in enterprise environments, not just in controlled demonstrations.

Enterprise AI doesn’t have to be complicated to be powerful. Sometimes the most transformative technology is the one that simply works as expected — reliably and at lightning speed.
