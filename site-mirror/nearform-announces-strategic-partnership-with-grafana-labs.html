Nearform announces strategic partnership with Grafana LabsNearform announces strategic partnership with Grafana LabsNearform announces strategic partnership with Grafana Labs

Nearform partners with Grafana Labs, the premier open observability platform, to help clients achieve greater reliability and issue resolution for enhanced system performance

4 Apr 2024
  • Share

A significant step forward

This partnership is a significant step forward in Nearform’s commitment to empowering clients to unlock even greater value from their data.

By uniting Nearform’s expertise in delivering resilient, customer-centric digital products with Grafana’s robust capabilities in data querying and visualisation, enterprise clients will benefit from comprehensive system visibility that enables their teams to streamline processes, increase efficiency, and foster a culture of proactive observability.

Observability is fundamental to enterprise growth

Whether it’s for a process that’s embedded in an organisation’s culture, or integrated within Dev Ops or Site Reliability Engineering (SRE), observability is best practice for technology teams. It ensures enterprises are able to monitor and minimise system downtime, and improve incident response times, resulting in a better user experience. 

Grafana’s intuitive, custom-built dashboards complement Nearform’s customer-centric digital solutions, and together the collaboration will enable Nearform clients to have a better understanding of their data, driving more informed decision-making that aligns technical action with strategic impact.

"Nearform wants to make the world better"

Commenting on the announcement, Chris Williams, Head of Strategic Partnerships at Nearform, says: “Nearform wants to make the world better by creating great customer, business and developer experiences, and partnering with Grafana Labs is a transformative next step in that journey. We are very excited about the collaborative work our two organisations will bring to customers as part of their journey towards Observability Maturity. Together, we have already made some impressive strides forward in our shared mission of making technology more intuitive and more reliable for our shared clients.”

"Grafana Labs is thrilled to join forces with Nearform"

 "Grafana Labs is thrilled to join forces with Nearform, leveraging our shared dedication to advancing organisations' observability practices," said Tom Kennedy, Director, EMEA and APAC Partnerships, Grafana Labs. "This strategic partnership combines the power of Grafana Cloud with Nearform's technical and business expertise to make it easier and faster for customers to get up and running with observability. Together, we're looking forward to helping organisations navigate the growing complexities of today's cloud-native world."

Working closely together to empower enterprises

Nearform and Grafana are committed to working closely together to empower enterprises to build enduring impact. To learn more about the partnership and how it can benefit your organisation, reach out to our team.
