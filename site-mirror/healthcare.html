Healthcare, life sciences, & public sectorHealthcare, life sciences, & public sector_Healthcare, life sciences, & public sector_

Connected data foundations and AI-enabled efficiencies play a critical role in healthcare and life sciences. These technologies are essential to improving both provider and patient outcomes. Nearform partners with the world’s leading healthcare organisations and government agencies to launch compliant, intelligent products that address both regulatory complexity and emerging needs, when the solution is a matter of life or death.

Improving quality of life through intelligent health services

With data playing an ever-growing role in delivering care, powering research and ensuring widespread healthcare access, data engineering is an essential capability to unlock insights. But the systems that support research and patient outcomes are often outdated and struggle to keep up with demand.

In partnering with Nearform, healthcare organisations connect data to identify new analytics opportunities, while releasing new products more quickly through AI-enabled software development. Whether improving accessibility for patients, clinical research or earlier interventions, Nearform brings a track record of success in applying data and AI capabilities to improve healthcare experiences.
