GROQD

A type-safe query builder for GROQ

Designed to give the best GROQ developer experience possible, with the flexibility of GROQ, schema-aware auto-completion, strong type-checking, and the runtime safety of Zod.

Features

  • Flexible
    GROQD maintains the flexibility of GROQ.
  • Type Safe
    Leverages the type safety of TypeScript.
  • Runtime Safe
    Automatically layers in the runtime safety of Zod.
